# Product Vision of "Old Book Wisenheimer"

Old Book Wisenheimer is a literature quiz focusing on quotes from old books.

The player's task is to guess the correct book for a given quote from several options over multiple rounds.

In the game results, affiliate links to bookstores are provided for every book mentioned in the game.
These links are intended to generate revenue.

Fun factors - players can:
- test and improve their knowledge of literature
- get to know interesting books and quotes
